# 2025-2026 Admissions Decision Timeline

*Last updated: February 2026*

## Quick Reference: Key Dates

| Date | Event | Priority |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Mid-Feb 2026** | ED2 decisions (Vanderbilt, WashU, Emory, Tufts, etc.) | NOW |
| **Mar 14** | MIT Pi Day decisions (6:28 PM ET) | FIRST RD WAVE |
| **Mid-March** | Caltech, UC system, Georgetown, Duke | HIGH VOLUME |
| **Mar 26** | IVY DAY (all 8 Ivies, confirmed by Yale) | BIGGEST DAY |
| **Late March** | Stanford RD (typically Friday) | HIGH ENGAGEMENT |
| **Apr 1** | Georgetown, many state schools | CONTINUED WAVE |
| **May 1** | National College Decision Day | FINAL PUSH |

## 7. Most Active Online Communities

### Reddit
- **r/ApplyingToCollege**: 488K-650K+ members. THE dominant community.
- **r/ChanceMe**: Students post stats to get "chanced"
- **r/CollegeResults**: Full profiles from admitted/rejected students

### Discord
- **ApplyingToCollege Discord**: ~47,000+ members. Has #decision-dates channel. discord.com/invite/a2c
- **College Admissions Hub**: ~12,000+ members

### College Confidential
- 22M+ posts. Has "Regular Decision Central" megathread. Skews older/parent-heavy.

### Live Calendar
- **applyingto.college/decision-calendar** — community-maintained live calendar of all release dates

---

## 8. Admitted Student Communities (Post-Decision)

Communities form within HOURS of decisions:

- **GroupMe**: Most common for class chats. groupme.com/en-US/campus/
- **Discord**: Student Hubs feature. Schools create official servers.
- **Instagram**: @harvard2030, @yale2030, etc.
- **Facebook Groups**: Declining but still used by some schools.
